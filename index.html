MyNotepad - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01 November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyNotepad! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Our core principle is simple: MyNotepad is a 100% offline application. Your data is your own.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be crystal clear. MyNotepad DOES NOT collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or data from you or your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information: We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details.
  • Notes and Content: All notes, titles, and content you create in the app are stored exclusively on your device’s local storage. We do not have access to them.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app, such as which buttons you click or how often you open it.
  • Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, like its model, operating system, or unique identifiers.
  • Location Information: The app does not collect or use your geographical location.

3. How Your Data is Stored

All notes you create are saved as simple text files (.txt) directly within the app's private directory on your device. This data is only accessible by you and the MyNotepad app itself. If you uninstall the app, this data will be permanently deleted from your device.

4. Permissions

MyNotepad is designed to be a minimal and secure app. It does not require any special permissions to function, such as access to your contacts, camera, or location.

5. Third-Party Services

MyNotepad does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection. The entire app is self-contained.

6. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will notify you by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any updates.
